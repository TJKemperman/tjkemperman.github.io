Study Choice Activities

I took part in a few activities before deciding where to take my next step as an IT student. During my final year at Scalda, I went to a few 'Open Days' at a few schools. I went to the Associate Degree Academy in Roosendaal and participated in the Open Day there. Following that, I went to the HZ Middelburg Open Day to check out the HBO-ICT education there. After that, I decided to go check out the Open Day at the Breda University of Applied Sciences to check out the Game Development education.

Following the Open Days, the school itself hosted a day at the Associate Degree Academy, inviting students to get a more in-depth look at the ICT education it offered. It mimicked the Open Day pretty much up to a certain point, then we did a small group assignment to have us dip our toes in what the education was.

These were the activities I took part in to decide what college I wanted to follow. I eliminated the Game Development one relatively quickly, deciding that I didn't really want to go down that niche, having desires to see what a higher education level would offer me. I ended up picking HZ, because the shorter 2-year education of the AD didn't really fit me. I know that Project Work is inevitable in IT, but the AD would set us into that almost immediately, and I personally prefer the introductory 'lesson part' of education, to get a slightly breezier first part of the education.

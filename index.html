Motivation HZ

One of the main reasons that I chose my next education to be the HBO-ICT education at HZ, was to see how more advanced the learning would be, and how much it would demand of me as a student. At Scalda, I did not really feel a lot of pressure, and I did not see myself in a working life just yet. As such, I went to go to HZ as a good challenge for myself. To see myself working at a solid pace.

Aside from the increase in pace, I am very willing to learn more. Compared to Software Development, HBO-ICT offers a lot more to learn, especially content like Data Science and Computer Science Basics. I have never really had much of that, so that is something new that I am very willing to pick up.

A more general reason as to why I picked the ICT field, has to mostly do with my hobby of playing games. I personally wanted to know how games were developed, which lead me to originally wanting to go to the Breda University of Applied Sciences to do Game Development there. I decided against it when the assignment given to apply was quite large and I did not understand any of it. However, it had piqued my interest enough to want to backtrack a bit, and go into Software Development in general, to see how sites and such are built. This lead me to pick the Software Development education at Scalda in Vlissingen, which I finished, and lead me to the HZ.
